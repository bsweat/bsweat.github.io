Brandon Sweat

I'm a cybersecurity enthusiast, IT consultant, and photographer based in Southern Utah. My path into security started through years of IT helpdesk work — fielding everything from network outages to "why is my printer blinking" — which gave me a ground-level understanding of the systems that attackers actually target.

These days I spend my time working through security labs and CTFs, building out my home lab, and sharpening my offensive and defensive skills across Windows and Linux environments. I'm interested in network security, penetration testing, and OSINT — and I document what I learn here to give back to the community that helped me get started.

Outside of security, I shoot portrait and landscape photography across the canyon country of Southern Utah — Zion, Bryce, Kanab, and everything in between. I also take on freelance IT consulting work, helping individuals and small businesses solve the full range of IT problems, from hardware advice to emergency data recovery.

If you want to talk security, book a session, or need IT help, reach out at brandonsweat@proton.me.
